Katariina Mahkonen

Katariina is a versatile data scientist and software developer with audio signal processing background. She has a PhD from Tampere University of Technology department of Signal Processing and diverse experience in AI-based solution creation.

Experience

Katariina has wide experience in AI consultancy type work. Currently she works as an independent consultant through AI Performance Oy and as a staff AI scientist in Silo AI. Before these she has worked as a Machine Learning Developer in an audio solutions company Meluta Oy and as a researcher and teacher at Tampere University of technology. During her career she has developed machine learning and sensor data based solutions for several customers.

In addition to technical tasks, teaching has been a beloved task, and she has lead an ESA Navisp -funded project on heavy working machines

She has experience in algorithm design and implementation for many kinds of setups for various types of data, her specialization area being time series data and vibro-acoustics. In the work of developing algorithms for pattern recognition, classification, forecasting and modeling, she has utilized multitude of computational methods of machine learning and statistics.

She has implemented algorithms both for edge as well as for cloud computing environments. Working with Python machine learning and deep learning libraries is her everyday work. She has also experience in implementing AI-services as a private cloud server and in Azure Machine Learning environment. Often user interface and various different databases are part of implementation.
